Workplace Health and Safety

EGM Build and Transform is fully aware of the mandatory workplace health and safety requirements as per the WHS & Construction safety Act. We adhere to WorkCover regulations and bylaws applicable to the construction industry on each and every site. Safe Work Method Statement (SWMS) and tool box talk meetings are regularly implemented by our team & supervisors. We thrive to achieve a high level of safety standards by committing to safety at the workplace and engaging in site hazard control and injury protection programs. It is our utmost priority to comply with the builders site safety plan.

We have certain basic systems in place to ensure safety at our workplace:
  • Each employee must have a construction induction white card
  • Each employee is inducted according to our OH&S procedures
  • Every work activity has a SWMS and each employee understands and signs it
  • Every employee adheres to the PPE standards
